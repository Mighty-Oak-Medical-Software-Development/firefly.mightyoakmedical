Who is Mighty Oak Medical?

Mighty Oak Medical was founded in 2011 with the goal of making patient-specific pedicle screw guides
accessible to spine surgeons worldwide. Over the years Mighty Oak has built upon its initial product to
become an industry leader in patient-specific technology and complex presurgical planning. its products have
been used in thousands of complex pediatric and adult cases in X countries.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to be thepremier provider of end-to-end patient specific technologies for spinal surgery
by harnessing advanced data analytics and additive manufacturing to make surgery more sage and predictable.
