S2-AI Navigation Guides

FIREFLY ® Technology

FIREFLY® S2AI Patient-Specific Navigation Guides are designed for the S2-Alar-Iliac fixation technique, commonly used as the foundation for long spinal constructs. The sacroiliac joint presents numerous complexities that can lead to challenges in free-hand placement of these screws, but use of these guides and Mighty Oak’s highly-skilled preoperative planning reduces potential for breaching of the acetabulum and provides a stable anchor for spinal fusion.

S2-AI Navigation Guides

Features and Benefits

REDUCED SCREW PROMINENCE WITH BETTER ALIGNMENT

Compared to traditional iliac trajectory, placement of S2AI screws using the FIREFLY® Platform reduces screw prominence and improves screw head alignment with S1 screw heads without the use of a connector[1] as the entry site is typically in-line with lumbar and S1 pedicle screws.

LOWER REVISION RATES

Compared to traditional iliac trajectory, placement of S2AI screws using the FIREFLY® Platform reduces screw prominence and improves screw head alignment with S1 screw heads without the use of a connector as the entry site is typically in-line with lumbar and S1 pedicle screws.[2]

ENHANCED BIOMECHANICAL STRENGTH

The S2AI Screw trajectory has proven to provide a more stable form of fixation for long construct cases and better caudal anchoring points that can be utilized in both the adult and pediatric populations[3][4][5].
