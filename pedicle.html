Pedicle Screw Navigation Guides

FIREFLY ® Technology

The FIREFLY® Pedicle Screw Navigation Guide (PSG) is designed to aid in the placement of traditional thoracolumbar pedicle fixation screws. These guides provide navigational solutions for simple constructs with abnormal anatomies and multi-level, complex deformity constructs, all while increasing accuracy and reducing time and radiation exposure.

Pedicle Screw Navigation Guides

Features and Benefits

ROTATIONAL DEFORMITY

It is difficult to plan, let alone, identify levels of the spine in two dimensions when dealing with extreme deformity or scoliosis cases. FIREFLY® Pedicle Screw Guides and theour analytical 3D Pre-Surgical Planning software aids in the correction of these advanced rotational deformity cases.

PEDIATRIC SOLUTIONS

FIREFLY® PSG is the perfect navigational solution for pediatric scoliosis. These patient-specific guides aid surgeons in the precise targeting and placement of screws within the small pedicles and reduce radiation exposure for these young patients. Mighty Oak Medical has an exclusive partnership with OrthoPediatrics, providing a full service offering for all surgeons’ pediatric needs

MULTIPLE CONFIGURATIONS

Multi-level, uni-lateral and bi-lateral guide options provide versatility and compatibility with both complex anatomies and anatomies devoid of anatomical landmarks due to previous cases and pre-existing patient hardware.
